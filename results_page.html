Vanilla Cake Recipe

    Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup sugar
  2. ½ cup butter
  3. 2 eggs
  4. 2 tbsp vanilla extract
  5. 1½ cup flour
  6. 1¾ tsp baking powder
  7. ½ cup milk
Step 1
Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C. Grease and flour a cake pan.
Step 2
Mix sugar and butter together in a bowl. Add eggs and mix in vanilla.
Step 3
Mix flour and baking powder in a separate bowl. Add to the wet ingredients and mix. Add milk and stir until smooth.
Step 4
Pour batter into cake pan.
Step 5
Bake in oven for 30 to 40 minutes.
Step 6
Remove from the oven and cool.
